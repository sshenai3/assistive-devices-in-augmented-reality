Food Preparation Board

About Product

Product Code: NC28199

Buy this product here

Multi-purpose board for fine and course grating, slicing, peeling. The Food Preparation Board is an adapted cutting board designed to assist those with weak hand function, or semi-loss of use of one hand, in peeling, slicing, grating and preparing food. This high quality food preparation board is designed to help increase independence in the kitchen.

Summary

Materials

  • The Food Preparation Board adapted cutting board is made of sustainable Rubberwood mounted on non-slip feet.
  • Bowls are stainless steel

Specifications

  • Dimensions: 17.32 inches long, 11.81 inches wide, 2.16 inch high
  • Weight:6.17 lbs

Rough cost bracket

  • The product runs for around $94.95 - $109.95
  • Includes: Stainless steel fine and coarse grater, slicer/peeler and collection bowls

Specific Instructions

  • Use a damp cloth to clean the board. Stainless Steel bowls can be cleaned in dishwasher.

Fit/Compatibility

  • The Food Preparation Board is ideal for people with weak grip, poor hand control, tremor, or the use of one hand for slicing cheese, keeping fruits and vegetables in place for peeling and cutting, and preparing food independently.
