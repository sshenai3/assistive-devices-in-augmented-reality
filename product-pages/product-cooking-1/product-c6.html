Adapted Cutting Board

About Product

The Adapted Swedish Cutting Board is a unique cutting board with vice grip securely holds food for one hand meal preparation. This adapted food preparation board has stainless steel spikes that hold food for cutting or slicing. It also features a vise to hold larger food items, jars or mixing bowls.
